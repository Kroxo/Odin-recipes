Here's a recipe for a shake!

Shakes are beloved by many for their versatile taste

Do you have what it takes to make a shake?

  • Milk
  • Whey protein
  • 2 bananas
  1. chop the bananas and throw them into the shaker
  2. add 3 scoops of whey protein
  3. pour a hefty amout of milk
  4. shake it!
  5. Enjoy!
